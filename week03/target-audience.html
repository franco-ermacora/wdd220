Index Target-Audience Design

Audience

  • The young audience, between 18 and 30 years old is the primary audience, but also parents, merchants, and employees can take advantage of the Chamber of Commerce since it offers many services.
  • People who are willing to pay for a membership.
  • Non-governmental entities to contribute to the chamber.

Scenarios

  • Young people or anyone who wants to train and develop for their personal progress and contribute to the area (Rosario in this case).
  • The person who is politically involved, either contributes or also to publicize the political party they support.
  • Employers or employees to find opportunities for other people or themselves.
  • People from non-governmental entities to collaborate with the chamber of commerce to improve resources.
  • Anyone who wants to know the news in the area (This only happens if the source is reliable and trustworthy).
